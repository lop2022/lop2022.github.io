      Hunter biden laptop is real

Published on Nov 24, 2022 by Admin.

In a report published on Monday, CBS News became the latest legacy outlet to authenticate the laptop.

It has since been verified by multiple outlets, including The New York Times, Washington Post, NBC News, and Politico, and CBS has recently joined the group.

CBS News is the latest corporate media outlet to confirm the authenticity of Hunter Biden's tainted laptop. Kevin Lamarque (Kevin Lamarque)

"Hunter Biden, the president's son, will be a target for investigations as Republicans take control of the House, which means data from a laptop purportedly belonging to Biden could be crucial to the investigation. CBS News obtained its information not from a third party or political operative, but from a source who told us they gave it to the FBI under subpoena "Tony Dokoupil, a CBS News anchor, stated. "To determine its authenticity, we commissioned an independent forensic review."

CBS News then stated, as many other news outlets have, that there is "no evidence" that the laptop was faked or tampered with. CBS News claimed it wanted to "cut through the noise" before determining that the laptop, which contains troves of data, emails, and potentially damaging information on the president's son, is legitimate.

Republicans, according to the report, have raised concerns about Joe Biden benefiting from a 2017 email about a proposed business deal with a Chinese energy firm. According to the report, no evidence has emerged that implicates the president in any wrongdoing.

Following the report, CBS Mornings anchor Gayle King suggested that Hunter Biden should have just paid his laptop bill and saved himself a lot of trouble.

The admission about the laptop's legitimacy contrasts sharply with previous CBS News coverage of the scandalous computer.

In an interview aired just days before the 2020 election, "60 Minutes" veteran journalist Lesley Stahl laughed off then-President Trump's claim that Joe Biden was "in the midst of a scandal."

"He's not," Stahl replied gleefully.




"Of course he is, Lesley," Trump stated emphatically.

"No, c'mon," Stahl said, dismissing the president's claim before lecturing him, "This is '60 Minutes,' and we can't put things on that we can't verify."

Previously, CBS News dismissed Hunter Biden's laptop. (LightRocket/Erik McGregor)

She also informed Trump that the laptop "could not be verified."

On the campaign trail, CBS News correspondent BoErickson was met with hostile pushback from other journalists, including one of his own colleagues, simply for inquiring about the laptop.

"My @CBSEveningNewsreport clearly lays out warnings about Giuliani Russian disinformation," then-CBS News White House correspondent Paula Reid tweeted, attempting to call Erickson's question to Biden into question.

CBS News did not respond immediately to a request for comment. Hunter Biden was also given a platform by CBS News to promote his book in 2021, with an interview that focused on his battles with addiction and efforts to make amends.
