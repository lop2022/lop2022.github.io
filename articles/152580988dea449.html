      Kevin bradley new jersey

Published on Nov 24, 2022 by Admin.

According to authorities, an SUV crashed through the window of an Apple Store in Hingham on Monday morning, killing one person and injuring at least 16 others.

Kevin Bradley, 65, of New Jersey, was identified as the deceased.

Officials said mid-day Monday at a press conference live-streamed by Boston 25 News that the incident happened around 10:45 a.m., when first responders received multiple 911 calls from the Derby Street Shoppes, where the store is located.

"This morning was an unthinkable morning, and people are still trying to process what happened," said Plymouth County District Attorney Tim Cruz.

According to authorities, a 2019 Toyota 4Runner collided with a plate-glass window, traveling into the store, striking multiple people and pinning some against a wall. Cruz stated that an "active criminal investigation" is ongoing and that the driver was with police.




"I saw a big car," said one witness to NBC 10 Boston. "Many people were fleeing. Someone must have dialed 911... I'm not sure how someone drives that fast around here."

According to Hingham Fire Chief Steve Murphy, the incident necessitated the participation of numerous agencies, including 14 ambulances and seven engines, as well as the assistance of bystanders. According to a news release from Cruz's office, the surviving 16 patients who required hospital transportation suffered varying degrees of traumatic injuries.

A building commissioner was called to the scene, but there were no concerns about the stability of the structure, according to Murphy.
