      22 freeway accident

Published on Dec 09, 2022 by Admin.

On Sunday evening, Dec. 4, a California Highway Patrol officer assisting a driver on the 22 Freeway in Garden Grove was injured when an SUV collided with his patrol car.

Around 8:45 p.m., the officer pulled over on the westbound side of the freeway, near the Euclid Street exit, to assist a driver experiencing mechanical difficulties, Orange County Fire Authority Capt. Jose Perez told OnScene TV reporters on the scene. An SUV collided with the vehicles, trapping the officer, while he was inside his patrol car.

Firefighters had to free the officer from his vehicle, and he was taken to the hospital, according to Perez. The extent of his injuries was not immediately known.




No one else in the second vehicle was hurt.

It was unclear whether the SUV's driver was injured or detained by police. The westbound lanes were closed Sunday night while authorities investigated.
