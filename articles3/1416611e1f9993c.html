Published on Dec 11, 2022 by Admin.

Georgia's Senate runoff election pits Democratic Sen. Raphael Warnock against Republican Herschel Walker. In the general election in November, no candidate received more than 50% of the vote, with Warnock leading with 49.4%, Walker receiving 48.5%, and Libertarian candidate Chase Oliver receiving 2.1%.

Democrats already have a majority in the Senate, with 50 senators plus Vice President Kamala Harris' tie-breaking vote, but the stakes remain high, with Democrats seeking an outright majority and Republicans seeking to retake a key seat in increasingly purple Georgia.

Election Night in America on CNN Continued: GA Runoff Special Coverage will begin at 4 p.m. ET on Tuesday and will end at 5 a.m. ET on Wednesday.




You can keep up with the latest results on CNN.com and through our live updates. CNN's decision desk will monitor the results and make projections as needed.

Polls in Georgia close at 7 p.m. ET. Mail-in ballots must be returned by Tuesday at the latest.
